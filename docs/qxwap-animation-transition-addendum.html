Accessibility & Performance Rules

Reduced motionHonor prefers-reduced-motion: reduce and disable non-essential transitions/animations.
Mobile scrollMotion must never block vertical scroll. Avoid gesture handlers that prevent default scroll.
Stable layoutLoading/pressed states must keep the same size and not shift cards or buttons.
Property choicePrefer opacity and transform. Avoid animating height, width, top, left, or layout-heavy properties.
